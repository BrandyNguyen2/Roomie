UCI Roomie is a project web page, part of the 2023 VenusHacks Hackathon, that updates the UCI Libraries study room booking experience. To book a room, head to the home page and click on any of the available centers/libraries there. Alternatively, make a reservation by clicking on the “Reserve” tab.
